Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 11, 2025

Thank you for using NewsByte, a Chrome extension designed to deliver bite-sized news directly in your browser. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Information Collection

NewsByte does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from its users. All processing happens locally within your browser.

2. Permissions

We only request the minimal Chrome extension permissions necessary to display news content and notifications. These permissions are used solely to enhance your experience and are not used for tracking or analytics.

3. Data Storage

All settings and preferences are stored locally on your device using Chrome's local storage. We do not store or sync any data on external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

NewsByte may fetch news content from public APIs, but we do not share any identifiable data during these interactions.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. If we do, we’ll post the updated version on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at nirupam.contact@gmail.com

By using the NewsByte extension, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
